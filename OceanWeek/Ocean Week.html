The Sunnydale Marine Institute aims to advance our understanding of oceans and coastal ecosystems and to encourage the sustainable use and protection of marine environments. Ocean Week is a week long event designed to educate Sunnydale residents about the ocean and it's influence on the planet.
With a variety of activities for all ages and interests, there's something for everyone!
